FAQs

Frequently asked questions

How can I explore projects quickly?

Use the main gateway cards on the homepage and open each project in a new tab from the Open Project button.

How often is project information updated?

Project content can be updated anytime through the admin dashboard and appears immediately after save.

Can I request collaboration or consulting?

Yes. Use the contact form and choose Business Inquiry. Include scope, timelines, and goals for a faster response.

How do I report a broken link or inaccurate information?

Submit a Support request with the project name, issue details, and a screenshot if possible.

Safety Guidelines

Stay secure and respectful

  • Never share passwords, API keys, or private credentials through forms.
  • Report suspicious links or impersonation immediately.
  • Use professional communication and avoid abusive language.
  • Do not submit malicious payloads or exploit attempts.

Platform Rules

Usage standards

  • Respect intellectual property and project attribution.
  • Do not automate abusive traffic or spam requests.
  • Use provided contact channels for support and business topics.
  • Violations may lead to blocked access or ignored requests.

Dispute Resolution Process

A transparent, fair escalation path

  1. Submit a Dispute request with clear facts and relevant evidence.
  2. Initial response is provided with acknowledgement and ticket ID.
  3. Review is conducted and outcome is shared with rationale.
  4. If unresolved, one final review can be requested for reassessment.
